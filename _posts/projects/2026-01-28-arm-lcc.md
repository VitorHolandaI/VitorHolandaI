# ARM LCC

A script to visualize the [LCC UFCG website](https://lcc.ufcg.edu.br/) directly in the terminal.

## About

A command-line tool that fetches and renders information from the LCC (Laboratório de Ciência da Computação) UFCG website, making it accessible from the terminal.
