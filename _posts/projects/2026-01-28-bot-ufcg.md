A simple Discord bot built to help navigate UFCG's campus. You type in a building block ID — like `BlocoAB` or `BlocoBW` — and the bot replies with a photo of that block so you know exactly what and where it is.

Built in Java, the project was created as a practical tool for students who aren't yet familiar with how the campus is laid out.

**Stack:** Java, Discord Bot API.
